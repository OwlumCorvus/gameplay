# The Home to be Forgotten  
# Hut:  
A small hut with a variety of decorations. From a **stone bird feeder**, **flowers** of all kinds and a set of **copper wind chimes**. The structure of the hut is made of **spruce wood** and **spruce branches**. Just outside of the hut is a small **campfire** with an **iron framed sitting swing** laced with **leather covered vines** to sit on. In the back under an awning, a **smithy** can be found. adorned with all a smith needs. A small **anvil**, a **forge** and even a **quenching barrel**.  
### Inside:  
Upon opening the door you can see to your left a **small sitting room**, to the right the **kitchen** sits. Beyond these rooms we find the last two rooms. The left room appears to be the **bedroom** and the right is an **office** like area.
### The Living Room:  
The living room has an unusually expensive couch sitting on one side. A central table holds a small candle. Next to the couch, on a small stand sits a beautiful fplant as adornment.  
### The Kitchen:  
The kitchen is a full setup, with a wood stove, a stone basin sink over which a set of cabinets hang. It would appear the rest of the counter space is used for food prep. As the utensil hooks along the wall of the open counter would suggest. Found in the center of the floor is an island counter and cabinet storage.  
### The Bedroom 
The room holds a bunk bed with a chest of drawers at the end. A lavish rugs covers the floor, and a wardrobesits on the opposite wall. On the wall directly across from the rooms entrance is a table filled with smoothstones, flowers and two candles.
### The Office  
The office was scattered with many scrolls and books. Candles sat on every other open surface, some half used many puddles of wax. A desk and chair center the room. on all sides but the entrance are cubby holes and shelves filled with text.
